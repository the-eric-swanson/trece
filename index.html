About the Game

OBJECTIVE:
Clear the table by pairing cards totaling 13.
Tap two cards to pair them.
Only one pass through the deck. Choose wisely!

  • ♠ Kings: 13 (just tap!)
  • ♥ Queens (12) + Aces (1).
  • ♦ Jacks (11) + 2.
  • ♣ Any other two cards that add up to 13.
  • ♠ Win: + 5 points + cards left in the deck.
  • ♥ Loss: - number of cards left on the table.
  • ♦ Clean sweep: Clear the table with no cards in the discard pile. + 5 bonus points.
  • ♣ Perfect game: Clear the table without drawing a single card. + 100 points!
