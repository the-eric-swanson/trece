THE HOUSE RULES

OBJECTIVE: Clear all columns by pairing cards totaling 13. Tap cards to pair them.

- KINGS: 13 (just tap!).
- QUEENS (12)+ ACE (1).
- JACKS (11)+ 2.

- SCORING:
- Win: + 5 points + number of cards left in the deck.
- Loss: - number of cards left on the table.
